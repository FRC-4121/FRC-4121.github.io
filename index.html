    FIRST

    For Insperation and Recogition in Science and Technology (FIRST) is a non-profit organization that focuses on inspiring students in science and technology. They are also responsible for organizing FRC, the FIRST league we compete in.

    Visit usfirst.org to find out more.

    FRC

    We compete on the varsity level of FIRST's leagues: FRC. This yearly competition is focused on a six week span from kick off to bag and tag day, in which we design, build, program, and optimize a robot.

    Visit FRC's site for more info.
