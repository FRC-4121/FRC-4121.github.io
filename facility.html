Facility

One of our main goals is to make sure team members get the most out of their experience, which is why our fabrication lab is so essential. From 3D printers to saws to mills, CNC routers, etc., we have a lot to offer to these students. It gives our team the unique opportunity to learn how to operate industrial machines and acquire new skills that will stay with us for the rest of our lives. The fabrication lab is open to other students and to the community as well.
