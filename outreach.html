Outreach

Team 4121 members are always looking for ways to help serve our community and teach people about FIRST’s values. For the first time ever, a FIRST Lego League (FLL) tournament was held in North Canton, entirely set up and run by our robotics team. We held a demonstration of our robot at a FIRST Junior Lego League event near us as well, letting potential FRC members get a feel for what may be coming in their futures. Our main outreach projects have been centered around doing robot demonstrations to show younger kids and the community what it is we do and how we spread the message of FIRST. We have driven the robot and talked about our team at local restaurants, the Main Street Festival, the Stark County FLL tournament, the St. Paul’s Jr. FLL Tournament, and several school functions.
